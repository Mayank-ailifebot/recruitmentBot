

An End-to-End Recruitment AI Bot just a "screening tool"; it is an Agentic Orchestrator that manages the entire lifecycle—from job requisition to the first day of onboarding.

1. Project Objectives & Scope
The goal is to deploy an autonomous AI agent that acts as a 24/7 digital recruiter, reducing Time-to-Hire by >40% and improving Candidate Experience (NPS) through instant, empathetic communication.
PART I
Functional Modules (The "What")
    • Module A: Intelligent Sourcing & Job Distribution
        ◦ Auto-generate inclusive, high-converting job descriptions using Generative AI.
        ◦ Automated multi-channel posting (Naukri, LinkedIn, indeed, and industry-specific portals).
    • Passive Sourcing has evolved from simple keyword searching to Vector-based Semantic Matching. Instead of looking for people who claim to have a skill, the AI should look for people whose "career DNA" matches our current "High Performers."
The "Top-Performer" Profile (The Anchor)
    • Before the AI looks externally, it should build a High-Performer Archetype by ingesting data from our internal systems and or data - Performance Management Systems, CRM, and Sales Dashboards
    • Success Vectors: It identifies patterns in your top 10% of employees—e.g., specific previous companies, typical tenure before promotion, educational background, and even the tone of their professional summaries.
    • Skill Adjacency: The AI should recognize that if a top-performing Life Insurance agent often has a background in "Pharma Sales" or "High-end Hospitality." It adds these "adjacent industries" to its search parameters for passive sourcing
Internal Database Mining (The "Silver Medalists")
    • The AI should "recycles" talent from your existing Applicant Tracking System 
    • The Silver Medalist: It identifies candidates who previously reached the final interview stage but weren't hired (perhaps for a different role) and re-evaluates them against the new role's Fit Score.
    • Employee Referrals: It scans the "extended networks" of your current top performers to identify potential referrals before you even ask for them.
LinkedIn & Public Web Scanning (The "Look-Alike" Search)
    • Using Agentic workflows, the AI crawls professional networks to find "Passive" candidates (those not actively looking for a job).
    • Semantic Look-Alikes: It will need to performs or do a "Vector Search." If our top agent is Person A, the AI finds Person B who has a similar career trajectory, similar certifications, and participates in similar professional groups.
    • Predictive Churn Detection: The AI identifies "Passive Leads" who are most likely to be open to a move.
    • Signals: A recent promotion of their boss, a company-wide layoff at their current firm, or a "Work Anniversary" (a common time for career reflection).
Automated "Warm-Up" Outreach
    • The AI doesn't just find them; it should initiate the relationship.
    • Hyper-Personalized Nudges: The bot sends a message that feels human: "Hi [Name], I noticed your work on the [Project X] at [Company Y]. Your career trajectory mirrors our most successful leads here at [Your Company]. Would you be open to a 5-minute chat about how we’re scaling our AI-driven sales unit?"
    • Interest Scoring: If the candidate clicks a link or asks a question, the AI scores their "Warmth" and hands the lead over to a human recruiter only when the candidate expresses interest.

    • Module B: Cognitive Screening & Assessments
1. Multimodal Resume & Profile Parsing
    • The objective is to move beyond simple keyword matching to "deep understanding" of candidate intent and history.
    • Structured & Unstructured Extraction: AI must extract data from PDFs, Word docs, and handwritten portfolios or image-based certifications.
    • Video Intro Processing: AI analyzes candidate-submitted video introductions to extract "Soft Skill" markers (communication clarity, energy, and professionalism).
    • Anomaly Detection: Automatically flags gaps in employment or inconsistencies between a resume and a LinkedIn profile (via API).
Contextual Shortlisting (The "Fit Score")
    • Instead of a binary "Yes/No," the AI generates a weighted Fit Score based on three dimensions:
    • Hard Skills (30%): Technical proficiency and certifications.
    • Experience Quality (40%): Not just "years of experience," but the relevance of the companies and roles previously held.
    • Behavioral Intent (30%): Analyzes the candidate's "Career Narrative"—are they looking for growth, stability, or a pivot?
    • Bias Mitigation: The model must be "Blind" to protected characteristics (Gender, Age, Ethnicity) during the scoring phase to ensure DE&I compliance.
Asynchronous AI Interviews
    • Agentic Interviewer: A voice or chat-based bot that conducts a 10–15 minute initial screening.
    • Dynamic Questioning: The AI does not follow a static script; it asks follow-up questions based on the candidate's previous answer (e.g., "You mentioned managing a team of 10, what was the most difficult conflict you resolved there?").
    • Automated Summarization: For every interview, the AI generates a "Candidate Snapshot" for the Hiring Manager, including:
    • The "Vibe" Check: Summary of soft skills.
    • The "Red Flags": Highlighting any mismatched expectations (e.g., salary or notice period).
    • The "Top 3 Reasons to Hire."
    • Process Evolution: Manual vs. Cognitive Screening

    • Feature	    • Legacy Screening (2023-2024)	    • Cognitive AI Screening (2026)
    • Parsing	    • Text-only OCR (fails on images).	    • Multimodal (Reads videos, images, text).
    • Logic	    • Keyword matching (e.g., "Java").	    • Semantic Understanding (Context of skills).
    • Interviews	    • Scheduled 30-min human calls.	    • On-demand AI screenings (24/7).
    • Review Time	    • 5–10 minutes per resume.	    • < 30 seconds for full profile + summary.

Deliverables for Module B
    • Parsing Engine: Validated for 98% accuracy across 50+ document formats.
    • Scoring Algorithm: Customized weighting based on your company’s "High Performer" profile.
    • Interview Persona: A fine-tuned LLM voice/chat agent with empathetic and professional grounding.
    • Hiring Manager Dashboard: A UI that displays the "Fit Score" and "Candidate Snapshot" summaries.
Success Metrics (Acceptance Criteria)
    • Screening Efficiency: Reduction in the number of "unqualified" candidates reaching the human interview stage by 70%.
    • Candidate NPS: >4.5/5 rating from candidates on the AI interview experience.
    • Alignment: >90% correlation between AI Fit Scores and final Human Interview scores.
Module C: Automated Scheduling & Coordination
        ◦ Real-time calendar synchronization between candidates and multiple interviewers.
        ◦ Autonomous rescheduling and reminder nudges via WhatsApp/Email.
Module D: Pre-boarding & Document Management
        ◦ Autonomous KYC/Identity verification.
        ◦ Digital contract generation and e-signature tracking.
        ◦ Pre-boarding Bot: Answering "First Day" FAQs to reduce new-hire anxiety.
PART II
1. Technical & Integration Requirements
    • ATS/HRIS Integration: Seamless bi-directional sync with our HR platform DarwinBox
    • Language Support: Multilingual capabilities (e.g., Hindi, English, and regional dialects) for diverse talent pools. Support upto 13 vernacular languages
    • AI Firewall (Safety): Implementation of bias-detection filters to ensure the AI does not discriminate based on gender, age, or ethnicity

2. Performance Metrics (KPIs)
The success of the AI Bot will be measured against these 2026 benchmarks:
    • Screening Accuracy: >95% agreement between AI shortlist and Human Recruiter shortlist.
    • Candidate Engagement Rate: >80% response rate to AI-initiated outreach.
    • Autonomous Resolution: >90% of candidate FAQs answered without human intervention.
    • Cost-per-Hire: Reduction of 30–40% by eliminating third-party agency spend for entry-level roles.



3. Out of Scope
    • Final hiring decisions (AI provides a "Recommendation," but a human makes the "Decision").
    • On-site physical security and hardware (Laptops, ID cards).
    • Executive Search (Headhunting for C-suite roles remains high-touch human).
